Who We Are

25 Years of Building Tamil Nadu's Skyline

Founded in 1999 in Dindigul, Stackly has grown from a 5-member team to 200+ professionals delivering landmark projects across South India. Our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation has made us the most trusted name in construction.

Our Journey

From Local Contractor to Industry Leader

Started with small residential projects in 1999. Today we handle 500+ crore infrastructure projects for TN Government, NHAI, and Fortune 500 companies. Our ISO 9001:2015 certified processes and zero-accident safety record speak for themselves.

We have completed over 450 projects spanning residential complexes, commercial towers, industrial facilities, and public infrastructure. Our team includes IIT and NIT engineers, certified architects, and experienced project managers.
