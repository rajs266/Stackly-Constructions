FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for a residential project? +

A standard 2BHK house takes 6-8 months from foundation to handover. For apartment complexes, it ranges from 18-24 months depending on the number of floors and amenities.

Do you provide bank loan assistance? +

Yes, we have tie-ups with all major banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, and Axis. Our dedicated loan assistance team helps with documentation and approval process.

What materials do you use for construction? +

We use only branded materials: UltraTech cement, TATA/TMT steel, Asian Paints, Jaguar fittings. All materials come with manufacturer warranty and quality certificates.

Is Vastu consultation included? +

Yes, free Vastu consultation is included in all residential projects. Our certified Vastu experts ensure optimal energy flow and directional alignment.

Do you offer EMI options? +

Yes, we offer flexible EMI options starting from ₹15,000/month for residential projects. Commercial and industrial projects have customized payment schedules.

What is your warranty policy? +

We provide 10-year structural warranty, 5-year waterproofing warranty, and 2-year electrical/plumbing warranty. Annual maintenance contracts are also available.
